# Kleindl_et_al_2026_Production_and_Enrichment_Shallow_Freshwater_Systems/data

__FCE1294_Lit_Review_Macro.csv__

This dataset includes macrophyte dry mass, macrophyte TP standing stock, and macrophyte TN standing stock data collected from 220 publications discovered during a literature review to quantify the relationship between planktonic or benthic producer (algae, cyanobacteria, and macrophyte) production and TP concentrations in water or producer biomass across P-limited shallow freshwater ecosystems. One dataset collected within the Greater Everglades, FL, USA is also included. Data publication spanned 1924 to 2026, and data collection spanned summer 1921 to May 2024.

__FCE1294_Lit_Review_Benthic_Micro.csv__

This dataset includes water depth, water column TP concentration, benthic algal chlorophyll-a, benthic microbial ash-free dry mass, benthic microbial TP standing stock, and benthic microbial TN standing stock data collected from 147 publications discovered during a literature review to quantify the relationship between planktonic or benthic producer (algae, cyanobacteria, and macrophyte) production and TP concentrations in water or producer biomass across P-limited shallow freshwater ecosystems. Two datasets collected within the Greater Everglades, FL, USA are also included. Data publication spanned 1966 to 2026, and data collection spanned June 1975 to October 2023.

__FCE1294_Lit_Review_Phyto.csv__

This dataset includes water column TP concentration and phytoplankton chlorophyll-a data collected from 58 publications discovered during a literature review to quantify the relationship between planktonic or benthic producer (algae, cyanobacteria, and macrophyte) production and TP concentrations in water or producer biomass across P-limited shallow freshwater ecosystems. Data publication spanned 1985 to 2025, and data collection spanned August 1988 to October 2023.

__FCE1294_Ever_Case_Study.csv__

This dataset includes water depth, benthic microbial dry mass, ash-free-dry mass, chlorophyll-a, TP and TN concentration and standing stock, total macrophyte dry mass and TP and TN concentration and standing stock, Cladium jamaicense dry mass and TP and TN concentration and standing stock, Eleocharis cellulosa dry mass and TP and TN concentration and standing stock, and total producer biomass and TP and TN concentration and standing stock found within each plot replicate sampled in ridge and slough habitats found at three Shark River Slough and three Taylor Slough/Panhandle wetland sites in Everglades National Park, FL, USA. Data collection spanned 2021-06-14 to 2023-06-27, and each plot was sampled once.
